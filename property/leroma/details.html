A premier, classic, heritage property near you, the only one of its kind in Yelahanka, just 20 minutes away from Bangalore International Airport. A boutique getaway location, that will give you an experience of royalty and luxury with it's eclectic, tasteful, decor and impeccable service that resonates with the architecture and culture of the oldest, historically richest countries of Asia. This private and exclusive villa exudes an air of sophistication and luxury, which is matched only by the royal treatment meted out by our experienced staff. Rejuvenate your senses amidst nature. Wind the evening down with a drink and dine by the Balinese Garden under the starry skies or simply soak in the charm and sounds of nature in this sprawling 2 acre abode. Unparalleled culinary, wellness and entertainment experiences customised to suit your requirement.

If you can define luxury in terms of experiences, then this diamond in the rough would be a perfect representation! This sophisticated boutique venue is designed for exclusive retreats for small groups, family outings, couples, product launches, movie shoots or wine & cheese soirees. Its sprawling outdoor space with lush lawns is perfect for hosting grand celebrations like sangeeths, weddings, receptions in the most traditional way possible with its grand interiors and architecture.
